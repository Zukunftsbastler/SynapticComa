# Communication & Information Asymmetry

## 1. Core Philosophy

To ensure genuine cooperation and eliminate the "Alpha Player" dynamic, the game relies on strict information asymmetry.

Players inhabit different realities (Dimension A and B) and have hidden inventories. They may coordinate goals verbally, but must execute solutions silently.

The rule is:

> **Talk about the goal. Stay silent about the method.**

---

## 2. Board Setup & Visibility

### 2.1 Hex Grid (Hidden Environment)

* Players only see their own dimension
* They see:

  * geometry
  * hazards
  * Shared Unlock Nodes
  * collectible conduits

---

### 2.2 Player Inventory (Hidden Resources)

* Conduits are private once collected
* Players may never reveal:

  * shape
  * orientation
  * inventory contents

---

### 2.3 DNA Matrix (Shared Space)

* Fully visible to both players
* All routing is public
* All effects are shared knowledge

---

### 2.4 Shared Unlock Nodes

* Visible in each player’s dimension
* Require **both players** to activate
* Result is public (e.g. AP gain)

---

## 3. Communication Rules

### 3.1 Allowed Communication (The "What")

Players may communicate:

* obstacles on their board
* needed abilities
* AP usage (“don’t spend yet”)
* readiness (“I’m in position”)
* Shared Unlock timing
* system state (“we’re out of AP”)

---

### 3.2 Forbidden Communication (The "How")

Players may NOT communicate:

* conduit shapes or inventory
* exact matrix placement reasoning
* routing plans
* exact movement paths revealing hidden info

---

## 4. Execution Silence

When performing actions on the DNA Matrix:

* no explanation is allowed
* no future intent may be stated

All placement is a **commitment**

---

## 5. Shared Unlock Coordination

Players may coordinate:

* when to trigger
* whether both are ready

Players may NOT reveal:

* how they reached the node
* what resources enabled it

---

## 6. Design Intent

The system enforces:

* shared goals
* private methods
* coordinated timing

There is:

* no turn discussion
* no round planning

Only:

* state awareness
* cooperative execution
